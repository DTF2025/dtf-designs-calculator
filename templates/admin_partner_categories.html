How This Works
  • Enabled Categories: Partners can access these product types in their calculator
  • Disabled Categories: Hidden from partner calculator (customers still see all categories)
  • Use Cases: Temporarily disable Apparel during maintenance, or limit partner access to specific product lines
  • Safety: At least one category must remain enabled for partners to use the calculator
